Ranvijay Mahal: A Regal Experience Like No Other

Step into a world of royalty with the first-ever palace set in the country, designed exclusively for commercial and prewedding photo/video shoots. What makes it truly exceptional?

Timeless Elegance: A red sandstone courtyard, century-old pillars, a vintage fountain, and intricately carved stone benches transport you to an era of grandeur. Majestic Architecture: This set epitomizes royalty, with its walls adorned with statues and artifacts that tell the rich stories of our cultural heritage. Grand Scale: With towering walls that reach up to 45 feet, the sheer magnitude of this set amplifies its beauty and regal charm. Capture your moments in this one-of-a-kind palace and let the magic of royalty unfold.
